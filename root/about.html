EXPLORE ORISON SOLUTIONS

We are one of the leading customised software solution providers in the UAE and Middle East since 2003. We provide stellar software solutions for a wide array of fields, including Construction, Trading, Spare Parts, Contracting, and Manufacturing Companies, in addition to Real Estate, Schools, and Colleges etc. Our product range runs the gamut from Accounting, Inventory, Personnel Management, Production, Real Estate, and Schools, to comprehensive SMS Solutions.
